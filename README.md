# About Me:
Computer Engineering @ KTUN.<br><br>My interests are centered on AI, machine learning, and data science, with a particular focus on the deeper question of how intelligent systems learn, adapt, and reason in a more general way. I am less drawn to surface-level trends and more interested in the structural foundations of intelligence itself, the mathematical, computational, and conceptual layers that make meaningful progress possible.<br><br>At the moment, I am strengthening my Python skills and building a more rigorous foundation in mathematics, statistics, and analytical thinking. I value working with data not only as a technical process, but as a way to uncover patterns, test ideas, and sharpen the way complex problems are understood.<br><br>I also explore finance through a data-driven perspective, where uncertainty, structure, and decision-making constantly intersect. Long term, my goal is to develop the depth needed to contribute meaningfully to AGI research and to the broader effort of building systems that can learn and understand at a more general level.
